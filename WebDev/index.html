Since 1887

More Than a Century of Craft

Our story begins in a small East London warehouse, where Thomas Ashby first experimented with green beans shipped directly from Ethiopian merchants. He believed that great coffee wasn't made — it was found, nurtured, and honoured.

Four generations later, we still travel to the source. We visit farms, sit with growers, taste alongside pickers. We pay above Fairtrade minimums because extraordinary coffee demands an extraordinary partnership.

Every bag you receive was roasted within 48 hours of shipping — never pre-roasted, never warehoused. Because coffee, like any great thing, deserves to be experienced at its best.

From Seed to Cup

How We Do It

I

Source

We travel to farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, Indonesia, Guatemala, Kenya, and Brazil — tasting, selecting, and forging direct partnerships with growers who share our values.

II

Roast

Back in our East London roastery, every batch is drum-roasted to order. No shortcuts, no blending — just the bean, the heat, and a century of expertise.

III

Ship

Your bag is packed within hours of roasting and dispatched the same day. Peak flavour, guaranteed. We call it "Roaster Fresh" — you'll taste the difference immediately.
